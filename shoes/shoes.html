Help

What do you need these shoes for?

Casual Wear

This option is for people who need shoes for doing every day things like going to work, walking, shopping etc.

Support

This option is for people who find regular shoes uncomfortable, or who have a medical condition that requires shoes that provide more support than others.

Night Out

This option is for people who want a good looking pair of shoes for the occasional night out.

Running

This option is for people who need shoes for running. These are usually sneakers and are made to withstand the wear and tear of everyday running.

Help

How much would you like to spend?

This one is pretty simple. Choose the amount that you would prefer to spend on a pair of shoes.

€30-€50

The cheapest option for a pair of shoes. Very cheap quality.

€50-€70

Getting there. Usually a good price to pay for a pair of quality childrens shoes.

€70-€100

The standard price to pay for a good pair of sneakers from brands like Nike and adidas.

€100-€120

Slighly above standard quality and aesthetics. Normal price for popular shoes from brands like Nike or adidas.

€120-€150

Above average price. Usually specialised shoes, designed to be more comfortable for activities like running.

€150-€180 & €180+

Very high price for very high quality shoes. Usually designer of strongly sought after.
