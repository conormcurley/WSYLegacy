FAQ.

Do you store my info?

Great question! Unless you create an account, we don’t store any of your personal information.

If you do create an account, we will only ask you for very basic information such as where you live, what your name is, how old you are. This is so we can tailor our quizzes to you personally and improve them from your point of view.

What is What Suits You?

What suits you is a service that helps people cut out the research aspect of finding a new computer, phone, pair of shoes etc. We provide you with a quiz (a selection of questions that help us identify what you’re looking for), and when you complete the quiz, we’ll give you one product that fits your needs perfectly. It’s as simple as that.

What do you ask in a quiz?

The quizzes are designed for people who might not know a lot about what they are looking for. For this reason, the quizzes are really simple, like: how much would you like to spend and how big would you like the screen to be. Furthermore, incase these questions are still to vague, there is a button at the top of each quiz to get more information about what the question is asking.
