About What Suits You.

What is What Suits You?

What Suits You is an internet service created to reduce the amount of time people spend researching a product before buying it. We provide quizzes that, on completion, will show the user one product that suits their needs perfectly. The user can either buy the product when they see it from one of our "buy now" links or save the product to our app, which they can present to a shop assistant at one of our partnered retailers.

Who are you?

What Suits You is currently run by two core members and many, many advisors. We also have part-time quiz testers to allow us to find bugs in our code before we release the quizzes. The two core members are the two founders of the company: Conor Curley and Michael Munnelly. They are both young entrepreneurs from the West of Ireland with a flair for programming, design and entrepreneurship.

Why should I use What Suits You?

If you have ever bought a new computer or phone, you'll know that it's a substantial investment. That amount of spending requires weeks of research to find a product that really works for you. We propose to omit this aspect completely. When we create out quizzes, we spend hundreds of hours researching that genre of product. When we finish our research, we boil all of the key factors of this product into five or six questions. This concision allows for the most accurate result without taking too much time. We've perfected our quizzes so well that we can pinpoint a product for you in as fast as two minutes.
