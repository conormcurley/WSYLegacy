Applying for an account - Submission of details

Applying for an account for WSYCode happens in two parts:

  1. Application for a business account. Usually accepted within one working day.
  2. Application for WSYCode. Usually accepted one working day after business account status is granted.
Note: You will be asked whether you would like to join WSYCode when you sign up for a business account. This means that part 2 of the above list is usually automatic and requires no further intervention.

Applying for a business account

Applying for a business account is really simple. It's very similar to a personal account, but it requires further information about your company, such as owner name and company domain (www.yourcompany.ie). We'll also run a few checks to make sure that your business is suitable for our service, such as an ICANN search and reviewal of your companies websites.

So here's what to do:

  1. Full Name - We recommend you have someone in a stable position of managment set up this account. Have them put their full name here. (e.g John Doe)
  2. Position - Have the person enter their position at the company here. (e.g Sales Manager)
  3. Company Email - Have the person enter the company email that you want us to contact you through here. (e.g sales@yourcompany.ie)
  4. Company Domain - Put your companies domain (i.e web address) here. (e.g youcompany.ie)
  5. Password - Here's the most important part. Make the password memorable, and store it in something like LastPass.

Just select WSYCode, then let's go, then you're done. Our systems will grant you a business account and then allow you access to the WSYCode service.

Once you are granted access to the service, we'll send you a link to select a payment plan.
