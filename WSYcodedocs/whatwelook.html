Payment - What we look for

We offer two different payment methods:

  • Business Credit/Debit Card
  • Business PayPal

Business Credit/Debit Card

This method required a business credit/debit card. All of the details required for payment should be available on the card itself. We look for the following details for a transaction using this payment method.

  • Card number
  • Name on card
  • Expiration date
  • CVV (Three digit security number)

Business PayPal

This method requires a business PayPal account. If you select this method you will be redirected to a secure PayPal payment screen, so we require no details using this method.

Note: if we do not recieve payment for WSYCode, we will send you an email telling you that payment was not successful, and that it should be fixed immediately. A week of grace will then begin, and once this week up all of your WSY-provided services will cease to work and your account will be frozen.
