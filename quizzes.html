Help

How does this work?

To begin, click on the quizzes section, and select a quiz that suits the product you need. Read the short blurb beforehand for a general idea of what to do. Go through the quiz, answering the questions that you feel describe your needs best, and remember, there are no right or wrong answers. When you finish the quiz, you will be presented with a product that suits you, and links to buy it from. The entire process should only take about 1-2 minutes.

What do the tags at the bottom of the cards mean?

The tags at the bottom of the cards specify the genre, development stage, and the time the quiz takes. We put these at the bottom for your convenience, but also to notify people about upcoming quizzes that they might be interested in. Don't let them confuse you, we're merely future-proofing, so in the future, our customers can find quizzes quickly thought filters.

How do I submit a quiz?

To submit a quiz, go to our contact us section, and select quiz suggestion and type your suggestion in the inquiry box. We try to process your inquiry as fast as possible, and if we think that quiz would be a good fit for our site, we'll ask our fans on social media. Soon after this, we'll try to find a partner and get the quiz up as soon as possible. Unfortunately, we cannot give you a definite timeframe on how long the quiz will take be up and running because of the variable involved.
